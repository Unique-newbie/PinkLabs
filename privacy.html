Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Information We Collect

When you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address, phone number (optional), project type, budget range, and message. This information is stored securely in our database to respond to your inquiry.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your project inquiries
  • Provide quotes and proposals
  • Communicate about ongoing projects

We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Theme preference — remembering your light/dark mode choice
  • Cookie consent — remembering your cookie acceptance choice

We do not use analytics cookies or tracking pixels.

Data Security

Your data is stored in a secure database with Row Level Security (RLS) policies. Only authenticated administrators can access contact submissions. All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email address listed on our Reach Out page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
